I am passionate about bridging the gap between hardware constraints and algorithmic efficiency to build scalable serving systems.

Currently, I am an Undergraduate Research Intern at **SCALE Lab** (Advisor: Prof. Jungho Ahn), working on speculative decoding and lightweight serving engines.
